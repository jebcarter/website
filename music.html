Musical Work

Music is incredibly important to me. I play guitar & piano and I sing with two groups at Princeton, Roaring 20 and the Princeton Chapel Choir. The first of these groups, Roaring 20, is a Co-Ed a cappella group for which I am Tour Manager and Social Media Chair. R20 has around 18 members and we sing a diverse repertoire from jazz pieces to classic oldies to today's pop. The second, Chapel Choir, is a group of around 80 singers who perform for the Princeton Chapel's non-denominational worship service every Sunday on campus.

M U S I C

Musical Work

Music is incredibly important to me. I play guitar & piano and I sing with two groups at Princeton, Roaring 20 and the Princeton Chapel Choir. The first of these groups, Roaring 20, is Princeton's premier Co-Ed a cappella group. The second, Princeton Chapel Choir, is a group of around 80 singers who perform for the Princeton Chapel's non-denominational worship service every Sunday on campus.

Roaring 20

Roaring 20, for which I sing tenor, has 15-20 members; we sing a diverse repertoire from jazz pieces to classic oldies to today's pop. I serve as Tour Manager and Social Media Chair for R20. As such, I am responsible for organizing and booking travel, housing, food, events, and performances for 15-20 singers on both domestic and international tours. I also manage all social media accounts for the group. In addition to handling several organizational- and publicity-related tasks, I also write musical arrangements for the group.

We sing dozens of performances each year, ranging from arch sings to concerts to professional gigs in cities across the world. See our most recent music video, a mash-up of three Justin Timberlake songs (below).

Also check us out on our website, Facebook, Spotify, Youtube, and Instagram (@princetonroaring20).

Chapel Choir

The Chapel Choir is a wonderful group of musicians and a close-knit, albeit large, family. We sing for the non-denominational church service in the beautiful Princeton Chapel every Sunday morning as well as two concerts every year, one in the fall and one in the spring. Recent concerts have included singing pieces by Mozart, Bach, and Brahms. We've had the privelege of being recorded professionally for a piece in a holiday Christmas album and we boast the largest rubber duck collection in all of New Jersey! Singing with the Chapel Choir is an absolute joy, and one of my favorite activities on campus. I'm also fortunate that Chapel Choir is an on-campus job. As such, all singers are paid an hourly wage for both rehearsals and sunday services as employees of the Office of Religious Life.

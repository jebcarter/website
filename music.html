Musical Work

Music is incredibly important to me. I play guitar & piano and I sing with two groups at Princeton, Roaring 20 and the Princeton Chapel Choir. The first of these groups, Roaring 20, is Princeton's premiere Co-Ed a cappella group. The second, Princeton Chapel Choir, is a group of around 80 singers who perform for the Princeton Chapel's non-denominational worship service every Sunday on campus.

Roaring 20

Roaring 20, for which I sing tenor, has anywhere from 15-20 members; we sing a diverse repertoire from jazz pieces to classic oldies to today's pop. I serve as Tour Manager and Social Media Chair for R20. As such, I am responsible for organizing and booking travel, housing, food, events, and performances for 15-20 singers on both domestic and international tours. I also manage all social media accounts for the group. In addition to handling several organizational- and publicity-related tasks, I also write musical arrangements for the group.
